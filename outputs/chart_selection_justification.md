# Chart Selection Justification

## Sales Trend View

### Chart Type
Line Chart

### Reason
Line charts effectively display changes in sales over time and help identify growth patterns, trends, and seasonal variations.

### Business Question
How have sales changed over time?

---

## Regional Performance View

### Chart Type
Horizontal Bar Chart

### Reason
Bar charts allow quick comparison of sales performance across different regions.

### Business Question
Which regions generate the highest sales?

---

## Category Profitability View

### Chart Type
Horizontal Bar Chart

### Reason
Profitability comparisons across multiple sub-categories are easily understood using horizontal bars.

### Business Question
Which product categories contribute the most profit?

---

## Customer Segment View

### Chart Type
Bar Chart

### Reason
Bar charts provide a clear comparison of sales generated by different customer segments.

### Business Question
Which customer segment contributes the highest sales?

---

## Shipping Performance View

### Chart Type
Bar Chart

### Reason
Average delivery times can be easily compared across shipping methods.

### Business Question
Which shipping method is most efficient?

---

## Discount vs Profit View

### Chart Type
Scatter Plot

### Reason
Scatter plots are ideal for analyzing relationships between two continuous variables.

### Business Question
How do discounts affect profitability?

---

## Return Analysis View

### Chart Type
Bar Chart

### Reason
Return rates across categories can be compared quickly and clearly.

### Business Question
Which categories have the highest return rates?

---

## KPI Cards

### Chart Type
KPI Indicators

### Reason
KPIs provide executive-level visibility into critical business metrics.

### Metrics Included
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Return Rate
